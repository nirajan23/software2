Jhupadi House

A Thangka painting shop showcases traditional Tibetan and Nepali art, featuring vibrant, handcrafted Thangka paintings. These detailed works depict deities, mandalas, and spiritual symbols, each created using natural pigments on cotton or silk. Visitors can often see artists at work and learn about the spiritual meanings behind each piece from knowledgeable shopkeepers. Some shops also offer workshops or live demonstrations, making it a culturally immersive experience with related artifacts like statues and prayer flags.
